Government Support and Centers for Epilepsy in India

In India, various government initiatives and healthcare centers provide support for epilepsy diagnosis, treatment, and management. Here are some key resources and programs available:

Government Initiatives

  • National Health Mission (NHM): Offers free diagnosis and treatment for epilepsy at primary health centers (PHCs) and district hospitals.
  • Ayushman Bharat: Provides financial assistance for healthcare services, including epilepsy treatment, to economically weaker sections.
  • District Disability Rehabilitation Centres (DDRCs): Provides support services for individuals with epilepsy and other neurological disorders.
  • Rashtriya Bal Swasthya Karyakram (RBSK): Screens children for neurological disorders, including epilepsy, and ensures early intervention.

Specialized Epilepsy Centers

Several specialized centers across India focus on epilepsy care and research. Some prominent centers include:

  • NIMHANS (Bengaluru): National Institute of Mental Health and Neuro Sciences provides advanced epilepsy diagnosis and treatment.
  • AIIMS (Delhi): All India Institute of Medical Sciences offers comprehensive epilepsy care through its neurology department.
  • SCTIMST (Thiruvananthapuram): Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology specializes in neurological disorders.
  • Epilepsy Foundation India: A non-profit organization that raises awareness and supports epilepsy patients nationwide.

Financial Assistance and Subsidies

Patients with epilepsy can benefit from government schemes that offer financial aid for treatment and medication, such as:

  • Free medication at government hospitals and PHCs.
  • Subsidized costs for MRI and EEG tests at select centers.
  • Reimbursement schemes under Employee State Insurance (ESI) and other welfare programs.

Contact for Assistance

If you need more information or assistance, you can contact:

  • National Epilepsy Helpline: 1800-11-2222
  • Email: epilepsyhelp@gov.in
  • Website: National Health Mission
